# Housing Purchase Eligibility Policy Rules (Template)

> [!NOTE]
> This is a **template policy** based on strict purchase restrictions common in major cities (e.g., Beijing, Shanghai).
> Please update this file with the specific local policies for your target city.

## 1. Local Resident Families (本市户籍家庭)

- **Definition**: At least one family member has a local Hukou (permanent residence permit).
- **Eligibility**:
    - **Single**: Can own **1** residential property.
    - **Married**: Can own **2** residential properties.
- **Exceptions**:
    - Specific district policies may apply.
    - Inherited properties might not count towards the limit (check local rules).

## 2. Local Resident Singles (本市户籍单身)

- **Definition**: Unmarried, Divorced, or Widowed individual with a local Hukou.
- **Eligibility**:
    - Can own **1** residential property.

## 3. Non-Local Resident Families/Singles (非本市户籍)

- **Definition**: No family member has a local Hukou.
- **Eligibility**:
    - **Base Rule**: Can own **1** residential property.
    - **Prerequisites**:
        - Must provide proof of payment of **Personal Income Tax** or **Social Security** for a continuous period of **X years** (e.g., 5 years) prior to the application date.
        - Must be **Married** (some cities restrict singles from buying).
- **Restrictions**:
    - If they already own 1 property, they are usually **not eligible** for a second one.
    - "补缴" (Retroactive payment) of social security often does not count towards the continuous period.

## 4. Corporate/Organization Purchase (企事业单位)

- **Eligibility**:
    - Usually restricted. May require the company to be incorporated for X years and have paid taxes exceeding Y amount.
    - Often cannot purchase residential properties in core districts.

## 5. Commercial Properties (Apartments/Lofts)

- **Eligibility**:
    - Usually **no purchase limits** for individuals context (but higher down payment/taxes).
    - Or strictly limited to Corporate purchase only (e.g., in Beijing).

## 6. Definition of "Family" (家庭定义)

- Includes: Purchaser, Spouse, and Minor Children.
- Adult children are treated as separate households.

## 7. Critical Questions for Eligibility Check

When verifying a user, you **MUST** ensure you have answers to these key questions:

1.  **Hukou Status**: Is the purchaser (and spouse) a local resident?
2.  **Marital Status**: Single, Married, Divorced, Widowed?
3.  **Current Property Count**: How many residential properties does the *family* currently own in this city?
4.  **Social Security/Tax**: (If non-local) Do they meet the continuous payment requirement (e.g., 5 years)?
